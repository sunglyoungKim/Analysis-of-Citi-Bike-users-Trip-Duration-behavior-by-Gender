Methodology

The null hypothesis is female Citi-bike users likely take longer trip duration than male Citi-bike users. The two distribution is not a normal distribution so T-test or Z-test is not very compatible to do the hypothesis testing. Therefore, Mann-Whitney U test is chosen to do the test because Mann-Whitney U test is taking the rank of all data by the total number of sample sizes.
